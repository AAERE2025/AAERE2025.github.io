VISA

If you need an official invitation letter for visa application, please fill in part 2 and 3 (二、三) of the following form and email to aaere.ruc2025@hotmail.com

China visa invitation letter

Nationals of 43 countries including Brunei, France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Holland, Malaysia, Switzerland, Ireland, Hungary, Austria, Belgium, Luxembourg, New Zealand, Australia, Poland, Portugal, Greece, Cyprus, Slovenia, Slovakia, Norway, Finland, Denmark, Iceland, Andorra, Monaco, Liechtenstein, Korea, Bulgaria, Romania, Croatia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Malta, Estonia, Latvia, Japan, Bazil, Argentina, Chile, Peru and Uruguay (Bazil, Argentina, Chile, Peru and Uruguay take effect from 1 June, 2025) holding valid ordinary passports can be exempted from visa requirement if entering into China for the purpose of business, tourism, family or friends visits, exchange and transit. They can stay in China for no more than 30 days without visa.

In addition, there are VISA-Free pilots for Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Sultanate of Oman, State of Kuwait, and Kingdom of Bahrain, effective from 9 June, 2025, valid until 8 June 2026.

The above information came from: VISA Waiver

Please verify the information carefully by yourself.

VISA application: https://www.visaforchina.cn
